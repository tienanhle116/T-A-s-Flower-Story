Our Story

This little flower shop was created from the love of Thanh Truc and Tien Anh. We believe a bouquet is more than a gift; it is a way to hold on to beautiful feelings. That is why we choose wax flowers and silk flowers that can stay lovely over time, helping meaningful moments last longer. If you need fresh flowers, we can still prepare them for you; send us a message and we will help you have the most beautiful fresh bouquet. Whether it is for your love, your parents, your friends, or anyone you treasure, we hope you find the perfect bouquet here. Thank you for visiting and growing with us. 🌷
